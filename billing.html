Billing & Subscriptions

1. What payment methods do you accept?

Card Policy:
We accept most major credit and debit cards, including: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, JCB, Diners Club, China UnionPay.

Cash Policy:
We accept cash if that is your only option. However, we recommend getting a credit or debit card for faster, secure purchases.

Payment Service Policy:
We accept PayPal, Cash App, and Venmo during in-person sales.

In-Person Selling Policy:
We sell at events, shows, gatherings, and farmers markets. We accept cash, PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, and custom orders (which require shipping and handling fees). We only ship to the US.

Other Policies:
We do not currently support cryptocurrency, checks, or buy-now-pay-later services. Contact support to suggest payment methods.

2. Can I cancel my order after purchasing?

No. We do not accept cancellations once an order is placed. Your item might already be in production or shipped. Please consider carefully before purchasing.

If your product is defective, damaged, or misshaped, we cover return shipping and offer a refund or replacement. Contact our Orders Support Team at orders@rekietalabs.com.

3. When will I be charged?

You will be charged immediately upon clicking “Pay” for your product and shipping. Orders are non-refundable after payment.

An invoice from REKIETALABS.COM will be sent. If you don’t receive it or suspect failure, contact our Order Support Team at orders@rekietalabs.com.

4. How do I get a refund?

Refunds are only provided if your product arrives defective or misshaped. Otherwise, no refunds are available.

Contact our Orders Support Team at orders@rekietalabs.com if you receive a defective item.

5. I was charged twice — what now?

If you were charged twice, contact our support immediately at support@rekietalabs.com with your purchase email or phone number for verification.
