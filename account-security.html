Account & Security

1. How do I reset my password?

If you have forgotten your password or would like to reset it, you can do that two ways.

First Way: When logging in, click the “Forgot Password” button located underneath the Password field. Provide the email used when creating the account, or the reset link will not send. Then you can reset your password using the link sent in your email.

Second Way: Head to your Account Settings, go to the “Security” section, and scroll down until you see the “Change Password” option. Enter your current password and your new password (twice) to confirm the change.

2. How do I change the email on my account?

To change your email, go to your Account Settings, head to the “Account Information” section, and scroll down to the “Account Email” field. Click the pencil icon to edit it.

You'll need to verify the new email before logging in again. If you forget the new email, please create a support ticket.

3. Why was my account banned or suspended?

Your account may be suspended or banned for:

  • Abuse of your account or services
  • Hacking or exploiting RekietaLabs systems
  • Violating Terms of Service
  • Fraud or suspicious behavior
  • Offensive or inappropriate activity

To appeal or request reactivation, please create a support ticket.

4. How do I delete my RekietaLabs account?

Go to your Account Settings, scroll to the “Danger Zone” section, and select “Delete Account.”

You’ll be asked to enter your password and confirm. Your deletion request will be processed within 24 hours. You can cancel during this window.

Disclaimer: All account information, models, photos, videos, and data will be permanently deleted and removed from our Marketplace.

5. I didn’t receive my verification email — what do I do?

If you didn’t get a verification email, there are two options:

  1. Log in at accounts.rekietalabs.com/mylabs/login. If your email isn’t verified, a "Resend verification email" button will appear.
  2. If that doesn’t work, create a support ticket and our team will help you out.
